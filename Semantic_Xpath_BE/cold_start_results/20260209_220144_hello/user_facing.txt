Quick replies to 'hello'

Three concise reply options in different tones so you can respond immediately based on context.

Friendly option
A warm, open reply that invites conversation.
- Text: "Hi! How's it going?"
- Tone: friendly and approachable
- Use with peers or casual acquaintances

Polite option
A slightly more formal and courteous response.
- Text: "Hello  nice to hear from you."
- Tone: polite and reserved
- Good for workplace or semi-formal contacts

Casual option
A relaxed, informal reply for close friends.
- Text: "Hey! What's up?"
- Tone: very casual and direct
- Use with close friends or informal groups
