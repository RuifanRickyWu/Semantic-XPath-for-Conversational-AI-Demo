## Examples

### Basic Queries
User: find items related to a topic
Output: /Root//Transport_Leg[atom(content =~ "topic")]

User: show me the second Day
Output: /Root/Day[2]

### Local Index Queries
User: everything under the first Day
Output: /Root/Day[1]/*

### Global Index Queries
User: the first 3 Transport_Leg overall
Output: (/Root//Transport_Leg)[1:3]

### Descendant Axis Queries
User: all Transport_Leg in the structure
Output: /Root//Transport_Leg
