7-Day Toronto Trip Plan

A seven-day balanced plan mixing landmark highlights, neighborhood exploration, optional day trips, and relaxed pacing so you can see Toronto without rushing.

Day 1  Arrival & Waterfront
Settle in and take a gentle waterfront stroll to recover from travel.
- Transfer from Pearson to downtown (UP Express recommended)
- Walk along Harbourfront to stretch your legs
- Dinner with lake views to end the day

Day 2  Downtown Icons
See major downtown attractions like CN Tower and St. Lawrence Market.
- Morning at CN Tower and Ripley's Aquarium
- Lunch and browsing at St. Lawrence Market
- Explore nearby historic streets

Day 3  Museums & Gardens
Visit cultural highlights like the ROM and choose a quieter garden or castle visit.
- Royal Ontario Museum in the morning
- Afternoon at Casa Loma or Allan Gardens depending on energy
- Light evening near your neighborhood

Day 4  Toronto Islands
Take a ferry to the islands for a relaxed day of beaches and skyline views.
- Morning ferry from Jack Layton Terminal
- Rent a bike or picnic on Centre Island
- Return for a calm dinner downtown

Day 5  Kensington Market & Queen West
Explore eclectic neighborhoods for shopping, street art, and dining.
- Wander Kensington Market in the morning
- Afternoon and evening on Queen Street West
- Try local cafes and vintage shops

Day 6  Day Trip or More Museums
Choose between a full-day trip (e.g., Niagara Falls) or deeper museum visits in Toronto.
- Niagara Falls for nature and photos or AGO for art
- Plan transport ahead if going outside the city
- Reserve time for dinner back in Toronto

Day 7  Flexible Departure
A flexible morning for last-minute shopping or a relaxed stroll before you leave.
- Light morning activity based on your departure time
- Confirm airport/train transfer and allow buffer
- Grab a final coffee or souvenir
