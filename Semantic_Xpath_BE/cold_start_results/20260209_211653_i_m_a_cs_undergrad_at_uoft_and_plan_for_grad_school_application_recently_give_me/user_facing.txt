1-Year Grad School Application Plan

A month-by-month checklist to organize preparation, applications, and follow-up so you stay on track from early planning to final decisions.

Plan and prioritize (Months 1-2)
Clarify your goals, build a timeline, and decide on required tests and prerequisites.
- Choose target programs and advisors
- Create a calendar with deadlines and reminders
- Assess need for GRE/subject tests and course gaps

Build research profile (Months 3-5)
Engage in research or projects and begin drafting a strong CV and statement.
- Start or deepen research projects with clear documentation
- Draft SOP and tailor your CV
- Prepare writing/research samples

Secure recommenders (Month 6)
Select and request strong letters well ahead of deadlines, providing materials to referees.
- Choose recommenders who know your research
- Provide CV, statement draft, and key accomplishments
- Confirm submission timelines

Polish and submit (Months 7-10)
Finish test prep, refine materials with feedback, then finalize and submit each application.
- Follow a consistent revision process and tailor for programs
- Pre-fill portals and verify uploads
- Submit early when possible and save confirmations

Interview and follow-up (Month 11)
Prepare research talks and conduct mock interviews; send prompt thank-you notes after contacts.
- Practice concise research presentations
- Prepare answers for technical and fit questions
- Send personalized follow-ups to interviewers and faculty

Decide and plan next steps (Month 12)
Evaluate offers based on fit and funding, and prepare contingencies if outcomes are unfavorable.
- Compare advisor fit, funding, and career alignment
- Negotiate or clarify funding when needed
- Have a reapplication or gap-year research plan ready
