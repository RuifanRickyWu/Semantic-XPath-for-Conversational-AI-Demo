5-Day Toronto Trip Plan

A relaxed 5-day schedule that balances major sights, neighborhood discovery, island time, and local food recommendations.

Day 1  Arrival & Waterfront
Settle in and take an easy waterfront stroll with skyline views.
- Check into your hotel and get oriented
- Visit CN Tower area and walk Queens Quay
- Dinner with lake views to unwind

Day 2  Museums & Distillery
Culture day: major museum in the morning and the historic Distillery District in the afternoon.
- Royal Ontario Museum exhibits
- Lunch near Yorkville
- Evening in the Distillery District

Day 3  Toronto Islands
Relax on the Toronto Islands with ferry rides, beaches, and easy cycling or walking.
- Morning ferry for skyline views
- Picnic or island cafe for lunch
- Return for dinner in the Entertainment District

Day 4  Kensington & Chinatown
Explore eclectic Kensington Market, enjoy Chinatown eats, and see local street art.
- Browse vintage shops and vendors
- Authentic lunch in Chinatown
- Afternoon art walk or gallery visit

Day 5  Market & Departure
Finish with St. Lawrence Market, optional quick attraction, then head home.
- Sample treats and souvenirs at St. Lawrence Market
- Choose a final attraction or shopping stop
- Allow time to collect luggage and travel to the airport
